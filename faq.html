We are committed to providing donors with a secure and reliable experience through our platform. Once you choose to give to a specific TT site, you can be sure your contribution will go where it is intended to. Here's how:

  • Only properly verified sites with site identification numbers are listed. With this identification number you can follow the progress of the particular site you've donated to.
  • Once a site is cleaned, you will receive notification and be able to view the before and after pictures online.
  • An on site representative from Toa Taka will be on the ground to ensure that all trash and debris has been removed from the site.

We hold a mandatory, mini-training session to educate workers and volunteers on the importance of creating and maintaining a trash-free,clean environment. We then implore all workers and volunteers to go out and share this information in their communities and with their friends and families. Once a site has been cleaned, TT places either one or two industrial trash cans (depending on location size) on the cleaned site.
